# CS255


The client of this project was DriverPass, they requested a system design to help support their clients as students taking their driving lessons.
I think I considered their requirements pretty well, but comparatively I would go back and revise the BRD to be better. I feel that it lacks actual insight into their business requirements which can be fixed easily with a bit more thought put into it.
Using the transcript from the interview I interpreted their designs fairly directly, it's important to keep their needs in mind when designing because they can change from business or person to the next. It's to be careful of not over-designing/over-engineering a solution based on their needs.
I approach designing software now with keeping more an idea of what the user's requirements are, alongside proper documentation through a BRD which can change throughout the process. It's a strategy among others that I've learned that I'll use when designing and analyzing systems in the future.
